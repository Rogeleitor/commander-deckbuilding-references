# Commander (EDH) Legality Reference

This document defines the authoritative rules and references for Commander (EDH)
deck construction used by AI-assisted deck builders.

---

## Official Format Rules

- Decks must contain exactly 100 cards, including the Commander.
- All cards must respect the color identity of the Commander.
- Singleton rule applies (except basic lands).
- The Commander must be a legal Commander.

---

## Banned Cards

The deck must not include any banned cards.

Authoritative source:
https://mtgcommander.net/index.php/banned-list/

This list may change over time and should always be treated as authoritative.

---

## Game Changers (Strictly Forbidden)

Cards designated as "Game Changers" must never be included under any circumstances.

Authoritative source (example):
https://edhrec.com/articles/game-changers/

---

## Commander-Legal Cards Reference

All cards included in decks must be legal in the Commander format.

Authoritative card database:
https://scryfall.com

Suggested reference query:
legal:commander is:commander

This document intentionally does not list individual cards.
Instead, it defines Scryfall as the authoritative oracle
for legality, oracle text, and color identity.

---

## Design Constraints

- Avoid infinite combos or deterministic loops.
- Avoid card combinations that generate infinite mana, damage, life, or cards.
- Prefer fair, synergistic interactions over combo wins.
- Avoid individual cards with a market value higher than 80 USD.

---

## Update Philosophy

This document is considered a living reference.
When Commander rules, bans, or format definitions change,
this document may be updated to reflect those changes.
